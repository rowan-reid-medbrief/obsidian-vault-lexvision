# Decision: MedBrief Match launched as first client-facing AI feature

## Decision

MedBrief launched MedBrief Match as a live product feature, making it the company's first client-facing AI feature. It helps legal teams find the right medical expert quickly, with AI specialty recommendations, a natural-language reason for each suggested expert, and a letter-of-approach generator. Presented by Chantel van Wyk at the Q1 company update on 2026-03-26 as a launch-day milestone, already live and piloting with LIME Solicitors.

## Rationale

Finding and instructing the right medical expert is a slow, high-administration pain point in legal medical workflows. MedBrief Match targets that directly and is the company's first move into client-facing AI, a strategic shift from internal tooling to a customer-facing product direction. It represents over 1,000 hours of work from around 15 people across the AI and development teams, and was presented as a fait accompli rather than a proposal, signalling that the launch decision was already taken.
